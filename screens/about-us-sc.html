This isn’t just a game.
This is my way of saying: I’m still moving. I’m still dreaming. I’m still chasing.

My name is José.
Earlier this year, I left everything behind and began a roadtrip across the United States.
My dream was simple: to see the world, to visit my brother on the other coast, and to feel free.
I didn’t have much money — just a car, a backpack, and a head full of ideas.

But the journey got harder.
I ran out of money.
My visa is about to expire.
And right now, I’m living in my car in Philadelphia — not by plan, but by necessity.
Still, I’ve never felt more alive.

I could have given up. But instead, I asked myself:
What if this became something bigger?
What if the end of my roadtrip became the start of someone else’s?

That’s how I created IYFIYKI — If You Find It, You Keep It.
A real-life game. A car hidden in the world. A story behind every clue.

And this isn’t just any car. It’s my car. My house.
The same car that carried me through cities, doubts, silence, and hope.
Now, it’s a prize.
If you solve the clues and find it, you win it. It becomes yours.

This whole game is my application.
